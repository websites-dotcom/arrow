GREEN ARROW aka OLIVER QUEEN

ABOUT THE GREEN ARROW

The Green Arrow, or Oliver Queen is the Starling City Vigilante, who catches criminals and brings justice to his city.

Oliver with his friends

His Characteristics

  1. He is a virile and disciplined man.
  2. He is very caring and passionate towards his loved ones.
  3. His passion and intensity make him strong, although he has always had difficulty finding direction in life.
